
## Coffee-Sales-Analysis-Dashboard

### Project Overview
This project analyzes coffee sales data using Microsoft Excel to uncover key business trends and customer behavior patterns. An interactive dashboard was created using Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, and Slicers to monitor sales performance, customer behavior, and product trends across a multi-year period (2019-2022) spanning the US, UK, and Ireland.

## üîç Key Insights & Analysis

### Geographic Performance
- **The United States** generates the highest revenue among all countries, indicating strong market penetration in North America
- **Market Distribution**: UK and Ireland contribute significant supplementary revenue, showing potential for expansion
- Regional preferences suggest opportunities for localized marketing strategies

### Customer Loyalty Impact
- **Loyalty Card Members** contribute a significant portion of total sales, demonstrating high customer retention
- Loyalty program shows strong ROI, with repeat customers generating consistent revenue streams
- Non-member conversion opportunities identified for revenue growth

### Product Performance
- Certain coffee types show **stronger performance across specific years**, indicating seasonal and trend variations
- Product mix optimization opportunities based on customer preferences by region
- High-performing varieties can be leveraged for marketing campaigns

### Customer Segmentation
- **A small group of customers drives a large share of revenue** (Pareto Principle - 80/20 rule)
- Top 5 customers represent disproportionate value - recommend VIP retention programs
- Mid-tier customer development strategy can unlock additional revenue growth

### Trends & Recommendations
1. **Growth Opportunity**: Expand loyalty program to increase recurring revenue
2. **Product Strategy**: Focus marketing efforts on high-performing coffee varieties
3. **Geographic Expansion**: Analyze factors contributing to US dominance; replicate in other markets
4. **Customer Retention**: Develop targeted retention strategies for top 5 customers
5. **Seasonal Planning**: Plan inventory based on year-over-year trend analysis
